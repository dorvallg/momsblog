I took a weekend trip to Newport, Rhode Island this weekend. What a BEAUTIFUL place. We stayed at Castle Hill Inn, in a Harbor front cottage. The service, the food, the view was fantastic. It is a very quaint mansion about 12 minutes from the center of town of Newport.

We ate dinner the first night at The Lawn, at Castle Hill Inn. I think I ate the best Lobster Roll I ever had!!!

The next morning we went to Cliff Walk, which is definitely a MUST. It provides great views of the mansions and the ocean, while getting some exercise.  We took a tour of the Breakers, one of the Vanderbilt Mansions, which is also a MUST see. I recommend using the app, which is like a private tour guide and you can move at your own pace.

We then went to town to have lunch and shop around. We ate roof-top at the Oyster Bar which was very conveniently located in town. Food was very fresh and delicious.

We had dinner at Thames Street Kitchen one night and the last night at the Black Pearl. Both I highly recommend.

The Castle Hill Inn served tea and treats everyday between 3pm-5pm. It was such a special treat. The sunsets were spectacular and breathtaking.

Great place to visit!! Please contact me with any questions regarding your itinerary or I can help you pack too :)

The DYSON AIR WRAP is definitely one of my favorite things. My hair has always been an issue for me to style on my own. I have a hard time blow drying my hair with 2 hands. The Dyson Air Wrap  has made it so easy. It comes with several different attachments in its own leather case.

The basic dryer attachment, is what I use first. It really cuts the drying/styling time in half. I dry my hair about 80%. I then use the LARGE curl barrels. You need to switch to do the other side. I then use the SMALL barrels to do the hair again, but very quickly. It takes about 15 minutes top to dry and style my hair.

If I want more hold in my curls, I also use the BEACH WAVER, 1in barrel. It comes in a few other sizes, but the 1in works best for my hair.
